ABOUT

What is the Collective?

Powerhand Collective is the apprenticeship arm of Powerhand Designs, a brand strategy & design agency rooted in African creativity and sustainability. The Collective exists to train, test, and identify high-potential creatives through real briefs, pod collaboration, and structured performance cycles.

This is a disciplined, performance-based engine designed to develop the next generation of strategic creatives especially women (18-25 years) across the continent.

What You'll Experience

  • 10-week structured pilot programme
  • Pod-based collaboration
  • Real-world brand simulation brief
  • Performance evaluation
  • Potential pathway into Powerhand Agency

Our Standards

Entry into the Collective is selective. Participants are evaluated across five dimensions that define what it means to be Powerhand-ready.

01
Technical Competence

Mastery of your craft and ability to deliver quality output consistently.

02
Strategic Thinking

Understanding the why behind every creative decision.

03
Discipline

Showing up prepared, meeting deadlines, and maintaining high standards.

04
Communication

Clear, professional, and intentional in every interaction.

05
Cultural Alignment

A deep resonance with the Powerhand ethos and vision.
