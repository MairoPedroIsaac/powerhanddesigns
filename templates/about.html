About Us

Powerhand Designs is a sustainability-driven branding agency for African SMEs. We solve two core issues: environmental waste from traditional branding materials and the lack of professional, affordable brand identity design.

Our mission is to empower African businesses to thrive through eco-conscious branding solutions that elevate their impact on both market and planet.
